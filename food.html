Explore the Flavors of South Korea

Bibimbap

A mixed rice dish topped with vegetables, meat, and egg, usually served with a dollop of gochujang (red chili paste).

Kimchi

Fermented cabbage with a spicy, tangy flavor, often served as a side dish.

Tteokbokki

Spicy rice cakes cooked in a gochujang-based sauce, often with fish cakes.
